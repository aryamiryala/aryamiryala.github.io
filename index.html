CineSync

RAG · ChromaDB · GPT-4o mini · Python

A retrieval-augmented compliance assistant for film production. CineSync ingests real regulatory PDFs from FilmLA, DGA, IATSE, and LAFD, chunks and embeds them into ChromaDB, and answers production-compliance questions with grounded citations — so crews get accurate answers instead of guesswork.

NLP · SQL/NoSQL · Python

A natural-language-to-database query engine that translates plain-English questions into executable queries across relational and NoSQL backends — letting non-technical users explore data without writing a line of SQL.
